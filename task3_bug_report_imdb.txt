Unable to Rate Movies with 10 Stars on IMDb's Top Box Office Page

Steps to Reproduce:

Open the IMDb website (www.imdb.com).
Log in to your account.
Navigate to the "Movies" section.
Click on "Top Box Office".
Select a movie.
Attempt to rate the movie with 10 stars.

Expected Result:
The "Rate" button should become enabled when selecting a rating of 10 stars, allowing the user to submit the rating for the selected movie.

Actual Result:
The "Rate" button remains disabled after selecting a rating of 10 stars, preventing the user from submitting the rating for the selected movie.

Browser and Version:
Google Chrome, Version 93.0.4577.63 (Official Build) (x86_64)

Operating System:
MacOS Monterey 12.6.7
